Ingredients

  • garlic infused olive oil
  • smoked paprika
  • cumin
  • lime zest and lime juice
  • oregano
  • chicken party wings

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 430 convection
  2. mix together olive oil, spices, lime zest and lime juice
  3. glove up and run into chicken wings
  4. place chicken in roaster pan and place in preheated oven
  5. bake 45 minutes
