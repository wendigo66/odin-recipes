Ingredients

  • frozen meatballs preferably IKEA brand
  • premade beef gravy
  • parsley
  • dijon mustard
  • half and half
  • Kevins Cauliflower mash

Steps

  1. cook frozen meatballs per instructions
  2. mix together gravy, half and half, parsley, dijon mustard. Place in small sauce pan over med low heat and stir.
  3. cook Kevind cauliflower per instructions
  4. Plate cauliflower, place meatballs on top and cover with gravy
