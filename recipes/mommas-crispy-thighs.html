Ingredients

  • boneless skinless chicken thighs/li>
  • smoked paprika, nutritional yeast, oregano, basil, salt and pepper
  • garlic infused olive oil

Steps

  1. trim fat and gross stuff from thighs, cut into bite sized pieces
  2. mix together all spices and olive oil and place in large bowl
  3. add chicken and stir to coat, can pop in fridge to marinate or cook immediately
  4. place in air fryer, 400 degrees for 18 minutes. Shake several times. When finished let cool 5 minutes to finish crisping
  5. EAT
